About Us

Restaurant

With an enviable culinary heritage dating back to more than seven decades the El Diablo Group has set new benchmarks in the restaurant industry. The El Diablo restaurant is a true icon of America and the El Diablo restaurant in MThe Marieta Islands is one of the most popular restaurants in the Mexico. The journey began in 1939 under the visionary leadership of Sudhakar along with his partner Anil who opened the first El Diablo restaurant in Washington DC, USA. The restaurant reached its position with the third generation of management at the helm – Joe Mathew and his partner Subramanyam. After initially offering a fusion of the Arabic region's Moplah and Thiyya Cuisines, the master chefs at El Diablo have ceaselessly innovated to transform the cuisine over the years.

The restaurants reflect the influences from different parts of the world while retaining their traditional roots. They today enjoy cult status in USA and Mexico, becoming a part of countless stories, memories and celebrations among our legion of patrons. Acclaimed as World's best restaurant for coastal cuisine(Times Now Foodie Award) and consistently ranked among Mexico's best restaurants(TimeOut Awards and Ahlan Masala Awards), El Diablo continues to set the highest standard of taste and service. We hope you have a El Diablo experience filled with unmatched taste, delightful contentment and memories to cherish.
